Frequently Asked Questions

Why is battery health not shown?

macOS imposes strict sandbox limitations on what third-party apps can access. Detailed battery health metrics are restricted by these built-in security protocols and cannot be accessed by StatsO.

Why are some system stats limited?

Apple restricts certain low-level system data, such as deep thermal sensors and raw component utilization, for system integrity, security, and privacy reasons.
