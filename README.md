# StatsO Support & Privacy

## About
StatsO is a lightweight macOS system monitor that provides real-time insights into CPU, memory, battery, and storage.

---

## Privacy Policy

StatsO does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All system information is processed locally on your device.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.

## FAQ

### Why is battery health not shown?
Battery health is restricted by macOS sandbox limitations and may not be available.

### Why are some advanced stats missing?
Some system-level data is not accessible due to Apple security restrictions.
